DETAIL LENGKAP PROGRAM STUDI FEBS

1. Program Studi: Digital Business (S1)

Fakultas: FEBS.

Deskripsi: Membangun bisnis digital Islami (e-commerce, fintech).

Sertifikasi: Digital Marketing Certification.

Kurikulum Utama: Digital Marketing & Branding, Islamic E-Commerce.

Karir: Islamic Digital Entrepreneur, Digital Marketing Manager.

2. Program Studi: Hajj & Umroh Management (S1)

Fakultas: FEBS.

Deskripsi: Manajemen travel haji/umrah profesional.

Sertifikasi: Certified Hajj & Umrah Manager (CHUM).

Kurikulum Utama: Hajj & Umrah Travel Management, Hospitality.

Karir: Travel Consultant, Operations Manager.

3. Program Studi: AI & Data Analytics (S1)

Fakultas: FEBS.

Deskripsi: Integrasi AI dan Big Data dengan Keuangan Syariah.

Sertifikasi: Asian Business Software Solution (ABSS).

Kurikulum Utama: Big Data for Islamic Finance, Machine Learning.

Karir: Data Analyst, Financial Data Scientist.

4. Program Studi: Islamic Finance and Fintech (S1)

Fakultas: FEBS.

Deskripsi: Keuangan digital, blockchain, dan manajemen risiko.

Sertifikasi: Associate Wealth Planner Syariah.

Kurikulum Utama: Islamic Financial Technology, Risk Management.

Karir: Digital Finance Analyst, Fintech Founder.

5. Program Studi: Halal Industry (S1)

Fakultas: FEBS.

Deskripsi: Inovasi produk halal global (makanan, fashion).

Sertifikasi: Halal Supervisor Certification.

Kurikulum Utama: Global Halal Value Chain, Halal Certification.

Karir: Halal Auditor, Policy Analyst.

6. Program Studi: Accounting & Auditing (S1)

Fakultas: FEBS.

Deskripsi: Akuntansi dan audit syariah standar internasional.

Sertifikasi: Brevet Tax Certification.

Kurikulum Utama: Sharia Auditing, Islamic Financial Accounting.

Karir: Sharia Auditor, Public Accountant.
