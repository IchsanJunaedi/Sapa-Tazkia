DETAIL LENGKAP PROGRAM STUDI HUMANIORA

1. Program Studi: Al-Qur'an & Hadits Education (S1)

Fakultas: Humaniora (Education Department).

Deskripsi: Mencetak pendidik Al-Qur'an & Hadits yang menguasai tahsin-tahfizh, metodologi pengajaran, dan teknologi pembelajaran berbasis nilai Islam.

Sertifikasi: Teaching Certificate.

Kurikulum Unggulan: Tahsin Tahfizh Mastery, Hadith Studies, Qur'an Hadith Teaching Methods, Technology Enhanced Qur'anic Learning, Islamic Education Counseling.

Prospek Karir: Islamic Education Teacher (Guru Sekolah/Madrasah), Qur'anic Instructor & Learning Center Mentor, Curriculum Developer for Qur'an Hadith Programs, Islamic Education Content Creator, Founder of Qur'anic Learning Institution.

Profil Alumni: Prisma Gymnastiar, S.Pd (PNS Kementerian Agama), Tsaisa Mausyaila Misbach (Guru MIN 1 Belitung Timur).

2. Program Studi: Journalism & Content Creation (S1)

Fakultas: Humaniora (Islamic Communication Department).

Deskripsi: Mencetak jurnalis, kreator konten, dan penulis profesional berlandaskan nilai Islam. Fokus pada narasi kuat dan konten multiplatform.

Sertifikasi: Content Writing & Digital Journalism Certification.

Kurikulum Unggulan: Islamic Journalism & Ethics, Storytelling for Digital Media, Photography & Videography, SEO & Digital Publishing.

Prospek Karir: Journalist / News Reporter, Content Creator & Scriptwriter, Digital Media Producer, Islamic Media Entrepreneur.

Profil Alumni: Ghifary Muhammad, S.H. (Live Commerce Specialist & Social Media Host), Muhammad Sayyid (Sosial Media Influencer - 400k+ Followers).

3. Program Studi: Islamic Family Law / Hukum Keluarga Islam (S1)

Fakultas: Humaniora (Law Department / Fakultas Hukum).

Kategori: Ahwal Syakhshiyyah.

Deskripsi: Mencetak pakar hukum yang menangani kasus perkawinan, warisan, dan perlindungan anak dengan perspektif hukum Islam dan nasional.

Sertifikasi: Certified Islamic Family Law Assistant.

Kurikulum Unggulan: Fiqh Keluarga dan Mawaris (Inheritance), Hukum Perkawinan dan Perceraian, Mediasi Sengketa, Gender & Child Protection in Islamic Law.

Prospek Karir: Family Law Advocate (Pengacara Hukum Keluarga), Religious Court Assistant (Asisten Pengadilan Agama), Mediator & Legal Advisor, Legal Educator in Islamic Law, Hakim (Melalui seleksi negara).

Profil Alumni: Nurul Syafitri, S.Pd (PNS Kemenag - Guru MTs Negeri 1 Pangkal Pinang), Rezza Haryo Nugroho, S.H. (Hakim di Mahkamah Agung RI).

4. Program Studi: Public Relation & Digital Communication (S1)

Fakultas: Humaniora (Islamic Communication Department).

Deskripsi: Membekali mahasiswa menjadi spesialis humas (PR) Islami yang adaptif di era digital, branding, dan manajemen krisis.

Sertifikasi: Public Relations & Crisis Communication Certification.

Kurikulum Unggulan: Public Relations Strategy, Islamic Communication Ethics, Digital & Crisis Communication, Personal Branding.

Prospek Karir: Public Relations Officer, Corporate Communication Specialist, Media & Social Media Strategist, Brand & Community Manager.

Profil Alumni: Maya Rosmala (Professional Master Of Ceremony), Addilah Sakinah (Social Media Specialist, Cinta Quran Foundation).

5. Program Studi: Film Media & Broadcasting (S1)

Fakultas: Humaniora (Islamic Communication Department).

Deskripsi: Mengintegrasikan produksi film, penyiaran, dan dakwah kreatif. Mencetak sineas muda visioner.

Sertifikasi: Broadcasting & Creative Video Production Certification.

Kurikulum Unggulan: Islamic Film Aesthetics, Directing & Screenwriting, Editing & Cinematography, Broadcasting & Studio Practice.

Prospek Karir: Film Director / Scriptwriter, Video Content Producer, Broadcast Presenter / Producer, Dakwah Media Creator.

Profil Alumni: Raksa Raga Firdaus (Professional Photographer - Ceritaraga), Rudi Rahmat Ilahi (Professional Videographer - Hunting RRI).

6. Program Studi: Commercial Law / Hukum Ekonomi Syariah (S1)

Fakultas: Humaniora (Law Department / Fakultas Hukum).

Kategori: Muamalah (Hukum Bisnis & Ekonomi).

Deskripsi: Fokus pada hukum bisnis, kontrak bisnis syariah, regulasi komersial, dan legal drafting.

Sertifikasi: Sharia Business Legal Consultant Certificate.

Kurikulum Unggulan: Islamic Business Law, Contract Law & Legal Drafting, Business Dispute Resolution, Comparative Commercial Law.

Prospek Karir: Sharia Business Lawyer (Pengacara Bisnis Syariah), Legal Consultant in Islamic Finance, In-House Counsel (Legal Perusahaan Syariah), Legal Compliance Officer.

Profil Alumni: Nurul Fadillah, S.H. (Analis Kebijakan Ahli Pertama di BPJPH), Tiara Salsabila, S.H. (Ketua Umum GenRe Kabupaten Bogor).
